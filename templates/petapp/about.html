About Us

Welcome to PetHomeBD

Bangladesh’s Online Pet Adoption Platform

At PetHomeBD, our mission is simple: to connect loving homes with pets in need across Bangladesh. We believe every pet deserves a family, and we’re here to make that happen by providing a trusted, user-friendly platform for pet adoption.

Important Notice

PetHomeBD is strictly an adoption platform and is not intended for selling pets. We strongly discourage and do not support the buying or selling of animals through our site.

Our purpose is to help pets find loving, caring homes where they can thrive, and we ask all users to respect this mission by refraining from using the site for commercial pet trading.

Who We Are

Founded by pet enthusiasts, PetHomeBD is Bangladesh’s dedicated online platform for adopting pets. Whether you’re looking to adopt a dog, cat, bird, or any other pet, PetHomeBD provides a place where animal lovers and pet seekers can come together, helping animals find new homes with caring owners.

Why Adopt from PetHomeBD?

  • Support a Good Cause: By adopting a pet, you’re helping to reduce the number of stray and abandoned animals in Bangladesh, promoting a humane approach to pet ownership.
  • Verified Listings: Every pet listed on our website is uploaded by registered users. Our team continuously monitors listings to ensure pets are posted in good faith and to reduce the risk of scams or commercial activities.
  • Diverse Range of Pets: From dogs and cats to birds and rabbits, our platform is home to many animals in need. We encourage users to adopt based on their lifestyle and capacity to care, ensuring the pet’s well-being.

How It Works

  1. Explore: Browse our listings to find pets available for adoption across Bangladesh.
  2. Connect: Contact the current caregiver to learn more and discuss adoption.
  3. Adopt: Welcome your new pet into your family!

Join Our Community

PetHomeBD is open to all pet lovers, whether you're here to adopt or simply support the cause. Connect with us on social media, attend local adoption events, or reach out if you have any questions.

Together, let’s make a difference for animals in need, one adoption at a time.
